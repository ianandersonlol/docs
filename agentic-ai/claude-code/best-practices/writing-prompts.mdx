The difference between a frustrating session and a great one is almost always the request. Good news: you don't need "prompt engineering" tricks — you need to communicate like you would with a sharp new analyst.

**What this page will cover:**
- Be specific about the outcome, not the steps
- Give context: what the data is, what it's for, who sees it
- Show an example of what "good" looks like
- Tell it what *not* to do
- Ask it to ask you questions when unsure
