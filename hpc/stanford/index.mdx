Sherlock is Stanford's primary shared HPC resource, operated by the Stanford Research Computing Center (SRCC). It features over 74,000 CPU cores, 1,100+ GPUs across multiple generations (H100, A100, L40S, V100), and petabytes of high-performance storage.
